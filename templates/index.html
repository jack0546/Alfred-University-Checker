WASSCE Grading System

Passing Grades
A1 - Excellent (1 point)

C5 - Credit (5 points)

Failing Grades
D7 - Pass (7 points)
E8 - Pass (8 points)
F9 - Fail (9 points)
Note: D7, E8, F9 are not counted as passing grades for university admission.
